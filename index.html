About Us

Greenman Gardens
Premium Garden Maintenance and Lawn mowing services.

Established in 2009, Greenman Gardens is a family business located on the Central Coast north of Sydney. Offering Premium Garden and Lawn Mowing services to the suburbs of Wyong, Hamlyn Terrace, Gorokan, Kanwal, Lake Haven and surrounds.

Our services include All aspects of Garden Maintenance such as Pruning, Hedging, Weeding, Topairy, Mowing, Whipper Snipping, Edging, Irrigation, Topdressing, Turfing, Pest and Disease control, Paving, Small Retaining walls, Garden beds, Garden makovers, etc.
